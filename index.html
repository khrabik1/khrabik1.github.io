My Research

I am a recent graduate from DePaul University, with a Masters of Science in Computer Science. I focused my studies in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, becoming an expert in such technologies as Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning, and Neural Networks and Deep Learning.

While at DePaul University, I had the pleasure of participating in graduate research with professor Clark Elliott PhD. During my time with the team, I explored leveraging Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning to help design solutions to complex problems in the industry, particularly in the realm of Emotionally Intelligent Agents and "Affective Computing".

I had the pleasure of working on neural network categorization of text input, as well as researching building a dialog and pragmatics manager for Emotionally Intelligent Software Agents. The research is ongoing, but an updated synopsis can be found here.
